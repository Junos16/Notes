### Perspective Process Model
- Waterfall Model
	- Requirement, Deisgn, COding, Testing, Maintaining
	- Benefits - Simple, Small
	- Drawback - Confusion, Requirement gap, Working only at end, blocking states
- Increment process Model
	- Well defined, linear, quickly
	- Benefits - Less people, risk management, quick
	- Drawbacks - degrade over time, not cost effective
- RAD Model
	- Extremely Short DC, component based
	- Steps - Requirement gathering, Analysis, Design, Construction, Deployment
	- Drawbacks - Multiple Teams, commitment, heavy resources, appropriate modularization, difficult adoption
