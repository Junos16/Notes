# Spontaneous Speech

- natural ability to express language
- opposite of prepared speech
- no need for prior planning
- no need for prompts

Ability to express thoughts, emotions, ideas without much effort (some sort of prior planning, requiring prompts, memorized phrases) and with high fluency


Richard Dufour, Yannick Estève, Paul Deléglise, Characterizing and detecting spontaneous speech: Application to speaker role recognition, Speech Communication, 2014, https://doi.org/10.1016/j.specom.2013.07.007
